send a photo, get a paint-by-number kit. 👋

you pick the photo, your dog, your wedding, the trip you still talk about, the house you grew up in. i turn it into a custom paint-by-number kit you print at home (or at any print shop) and paint yourself. no drawing skill needed: if you can match a number to a number, you can paint this.

WHAT YOU GET (a printable PDF kit)
• the numbered outline, lines printed light on purpose, so they vanish under your paint
• the swatch card, every colour in your painting, numbered and named, so mixing and matching paint is easy
• paint-along pages, the painting built up one colour at a time, so you always know what to paint next
• a full-colour preview of the finished painting, for your fridge or your frame

HOW IT WORKS
1. order, then send me your photo in a message
2. i check it’s a photo that will paint well, if not, i’ll ask for another (no charge)
3. your kit arrives in your inbox within [turnaround, e.g. 3 to 5 days]
4. print it, grab your paints, and go one number at a time

GOOD TO KNOW
• sizes from [smallest size] up to [largest size], tell me where it’s going and i’ll recommend one
• between [min] and [max] colours depending on your photo, more colours, more detail, more painting
• works with acrylics, watercolour, or coloured pencils
• this is a digital kit, nothing ships, nothing to wait for at the door

photos that paint best: good light, one clear subject, not too far away. when in doubt, send it over, i’ll tell you honestly.

etsy → messages → saved replies → “print help”
copy
let’s fix that, it’s almost always one of three settings:
1. print at “actual size” or “100%”, never “fit to page”.
2. pick the paper size your kit was made for, it’s named on the kit’s cover sheet.
3. if your printer maxes out at letter size, a print shop can do larger sizes for a dollar or two.

if it still looks off, send me a photo of the print and i’ll sort it out with you.

words we use / words we avoid
anyone writing for the brand
copy
we use: hello, photo, painting, kit, numbers, colours, print at home, one colour at a time, made by megan, beginner-friendly, your.

we avoid: ALGORITHM, AI-powered, automagically, pixels, data, generate/generated, render, output, hassle-free, game-changer, unleash, and anything in all caps or Title Case.

spelling: “colour” everywhere, always. lowercase everywhere, always (even at the start of sentences). the only emoji is the wave 👋.
the three rules
pin this above the desk
copy
1. lowercase always, the name is “hello paint”, never “Hello Paint”, never “hellopaint” (except in handles).
2. no orange, anywhere, not in graphics, not in photo grading. it’s the one colour the brand never wears.
3. show the before & after, the photo next to the painting is the most convincing thing we own. lead with it.
