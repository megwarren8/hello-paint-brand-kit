HELLO PAINT - BRAND SYSTEM
==========================

This folder is the whole Hello Paint brand in one place: the brand book, logos,
color, type, a full asset library, copy and voice, templates, and the original
source files. It is built to work fully offline and look the same every time.

Hello Paint turns a customer photo into a custom paint-by-number kit. The brand
is cozy and cheery, friendly to all ages, and made by a person, never techy and
never an arcade.


OPEN IT
-------
Double-click:  "00-START-HERE.html"

It opens in your browser (Safari, Chrome, anything). No internet needed, no
install. From there, the "Open the Brand Book" button opens the full visual
guide. You can also open "01-brand-book.html" directly.

Both pages look identical online and offline: the fonts and every graphic are
built into the files themselves, so nothing can load wrong or go missing.

FILE FORMATS
------------
  SVG   A graphic that stays sharp at any size (vector). Recolors with a fill
        change. Opens in Canva, Figma, Illustrator, or any browser. Best for
        print and editing. Most of the asset library is SVG.
  PNG   A fixed-resolution image. Best for a quick drop-in.
  HTML  The brand book and dashboard. Open in any browser.
  TXT   Plain-text specs and copy. Open anywhere.


THE QUICK BRAND RULES
---------------------
  Raspberry  #E64C81   The "hello." Headlines, shapes, the logo.
  Teal       #15A39A   Eye-catching accent.
  Sunshine   #F6C744   Warm pop and number tiles.
  Leaf       #5BA86B   Secondary accent.
  Plum ink   #312B3D   The dark. Body text and outlines. Use instead of black.
  Cream      #FBF4E8   Primary background. Lead with it so the brights pop.

  Type:   Fraunces for headings. Nunito Sans for body and the wordmark.
          Two fonts, no more.

  Voice:  Cozy cool girl. Plain over clever. Invite, do not instruct.
  Lines:  "send a photo, get a paint-by-number kit"
          "your photo, painted by you"   "no drawing needed, ever"

  Avoid:  Orange or terracotta (on purpose), Title Case or all-caps wordmark,
          gradients, shadows, pixel or arcade styling, body text in bright
          raspberry or teal, and stretching, rotating, or recoloring the marks.


KEY (how this note is written)
------------------------------
  UPPERCASE HEADINGS   Section breaks.
  "Quoted text"        The exact name of a file to open.
  Indented mono rows   Exact values to copy: hex codes, file names.
  Hyphen "-"           Used in place of a dash on purpose. No em dashes anywhere,
                       because they read as minus signs in STEM contexts.


GLOSSARY
--------
  Wordmark    A logo made from styled text ("hello paint").
  Lockup      The wordmark and a mark arranged together as a set unit.
  Mark        A small symbol logo (the bubble, the smile, the snapshot).
  Favicon     The small icon in a browser tab.
  Avatar      The round profile image used on social and shops.
  Die-cut     A sticker shape cut to its outline rather than a square.
  Watermark   A faint mark laid over an image to show ownership.
  SVG         A graphic that stays sharp at any size (vector). Best for print.
  PNG         A fixed-resolution image. Best for quick use.
  Reversed    A light version of a mark made to sit on a dark fill.
  Clear space The minimum empty margin around a logo so nothing crowds it.
  Offline     Works with no internet connection.
